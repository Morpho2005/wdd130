New Mexico

New Mexico is a state in the USA that is bordered by Texas and Oklahoma to the east, Colorado to the north, and Arizona to the left. New Mexico is a part of the four corners area, along with Colorado, Arizona, and Utah, notable for being the only instance of four state corners meating at the same point.
